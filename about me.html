About me

Hi I'm Lucy! I'm a friendly and enthusiastic individual based in the north of England. I studied music at university but my first graduate job exposed me to the digital world where I learned all about SEO, analytics, web design, and UX. I then worked for Heineken UK as an online project manager where I ensured our products had good visbility on our retailer sites. After 3 years of work I decided to pursue my goal of travelling and spent time in South East Asia, North America, Central and South America. My interest in the digital world was further inspired by others I met whilst abroad, so upon my recent return I sought out a place to learn more - Northcoders!
